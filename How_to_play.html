Basketball can be played and enjoyed with mates or competitivley at competitions and clubs.

Basketball is a team sport where two teams of 5 players try to score points by shooting a ball through a hoop, which is called the basket (or the rim, hoop, bucket), which is mounted 10 feet (3.05 meters) above the ground and is made of metal, it also has a net to make it obvious if the ball has gone in.

Basketball involves dribbling, passing, and shooting the ball to advance it down the court and score points. It's a fast-paced sport, with players using a variety of offensive and defensive strategies to outmaneuver their opponents.
