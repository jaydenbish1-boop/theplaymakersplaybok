About us

At the Playmakers Playbook our goal is simple, to be your go to guide for learning how to play anything, whether its a sport, board game or even a party game.

We know that learning new things can be difficult and confusing, so we priotise easy and simple learning instructions so its not one big leap but lots of small little steps. We make sure that whether your a beginner or master you can join in on the game and have fun.

Our playbook is all about:
Simplicity - Simple guides that are easy to understand
Variety - From old, classic sports to new, modern board games
Community - Helping all people connect over somthing we all enjoyable

So whether your learning the rules of a sport, picking up a new board game with your mates or trying to find the perfect party activity, the playmakers playbook is for you.
